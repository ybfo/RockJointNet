MINIMAL CAPTION TEXT
--------------------

GeoSPIN follows a PINN-style computation graph. Static descriptors form c, normal
stress is mapped to s, quadrature nodes u_k query online physics Q, and the
decoder C_psi outputs a positive rate r_k. Numerical integration gives tau_hat.
Boundary, monotonicity, and positivity are checked on the right; data and
trusted-region C-BB losses are accumulated at the bottom.
